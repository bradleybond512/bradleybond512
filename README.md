## Featured Project

### [World Monitor — macOS Native](https://github.com/bradleybond512/worldmonitor-macos)

> A native macOS geopolitical intelligence platform with 60+ real-time data panels, a live cyber threat map, AI-powered country briefs, space weather, disease surveillance, air quality tracking, and a 3D WebGL globe.

**What it does:**
- 🌍 **Live cyber threat map** — 500 IOCs geo-plotted in real-time (C2 servers, malware, phishing)
- 🚨 **Alert Center** — persistent history of intelligence signals and breaking alerts with unread badge
- 🔔 **Native macOS notifications** — OS notification center alerts for critical breaking events
- 🤖 **AI country briefs** — Claude · Groq · OpenRouter · local Ollama, with World Bank economic context
- 🛰️ **Space weather** — NOAA SWPC Kp index, solar wind, Bz, X-ray flares, geomagnetic storm alerts
- 🦠 **Disease outbreaks** — WHO Disease Outbreak News + ReliefWeb health situation reports
- 💨 **Air quality** — Open-Meteo global AQI for 18 major cities (PM2.5, ozone, NO₂)
- 🔥 **Satellite fire & earthquake tracking** — FIRMS VIIRS + USGS M4.5+ seismic data
- ✈️ **Military flight & vessel monitoring** — ADS-B + AIS in real-time
- 📊 **Market surveillance** — macro radar, BTC ETF flows, stablecoin peg health, ETF tracker
- 🗺️ **40+ map layers** — conflicts, bases, nuclear facilities, cables, pipelines, GPS jamming zones
- 🔐 **Security hardened** — bundle ID verification, CSP hardening, href injection protection
